# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Create a car shop application with a loading screen that transitions to a photo gallery showcasing cars and engines with pricing information.

**Planned changes:**
- Add loading screen that displays when the app first launches
- Create photo gallery displaying images of cars and car engines
- Display "Buy" text and price below each car photo
- Apply premium automotive showroom design theme with professional styling

**User-visible outcome:** Users will see a loading screen upon entering the app, then browse a gallery of cars and engines with visible pricing and purchase information, all styled with a premium automotive showroom aesthetic.
